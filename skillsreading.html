Visier People, skills reading game for employers and applicants

At Visier, I designed a skills reading game to introduce customers to a new product.

Visier's job and skills ontology is an API-based platform for strategic workforce planning, jo matching, learning, and recruitment. The platform takes your data and returns actionable insights on an organization or employee level.

Roles

  1. Lead designer, Product-led growth
  2. Illustrator

Duration

December 2022 to January 2023

Tools

Figma, Procreate

Project objective

The objective was to demonstrate the value and potential of Visier's job matching capabilities to employers and applicants while facilitating the collection of emails and conducting an information transaction to help build Visier's baseline dataset.

Fig 01. Tarot cards: Design and illustration

I led the design process from concept development, research, and testing through to the final product—a cross-platform card reading game to match talent aquisition and job seekers to skill profiles.
